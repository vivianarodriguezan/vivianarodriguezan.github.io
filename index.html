I am an Assistant Professor of Economics at the University of Texas at San Antonio. I received my PhD in Economics and Education from Teachers College, Columbia University. My research interests include the economics of education, labor economics, and cost-effectiveness analysis.

I am also an Affiliated Researcher at the University of Pennsylvania's Center for Benefit-Cost Studies in Education.

I currently serve as the Economic Evaluation Section Co-Editor for the American Journal of Evaluation. You can find my CV here.
